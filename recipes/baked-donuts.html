Baked Pumpkin Spice Donuts with Maple Glaze

When my family visited before our Halloween trip to Japan, I baked them some pumpkin spice donuts!

I followed this recipe from Katiebird Bakes.

  • I did cheat a little... I used a Betty Crocker spice cake mix as the base of my donuts! And added some of my homemade pumpkin spice mix for some extra autumnal spice.
  • I sprinkled autumnal leaf sprinkles on the icing of half the donuts.
  • And sprinkled Bird & Blend spooky sprinkles on the other half!

They went down a treat!
